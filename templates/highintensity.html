High-intensity interval training (HIIT)

HIIT workouts can be challenging, but they are also very effective. If you are new to HIIT, it is important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. You should also listen to your body and stop if you feel any pain. Some of the benefits of HIIT are improved cardiovascular health,athletic performance and mental health.It also burn calories,build muscles and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
