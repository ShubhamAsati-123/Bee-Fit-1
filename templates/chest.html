CHEST EXERCISE

While starting chest exercises one should use light weights and then gradually move on to increasing the weight and intensity of your exercise.It is important to warm up before starting any chest exercise.A warm up can include stretching of the body, short jumps, jogging/walking and leg swings. After the workout it is important to cool down which help your body recover. A simple cool down includes walking /jogging, static stretching and bending your elbows.Some of the benefits of chest exercise are that it improves posture as well as self esteem, increases the range of motion and muscle mass and also reduces the risk of injury.
