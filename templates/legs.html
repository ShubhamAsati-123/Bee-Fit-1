LEG EXERCISE

Some of the benefit of leg exercises are that it build muscle and strength in lower body which can enhance your performance in sports and other activities. It improves your cardiovascular health, body posture and balance. It helps burn calories, reduce weight and also reduces the risk of injury. It increases the mobility and range of the of motion of the leg which helps to increase your flexibility. It will boost your mood and energy levels as they release endorphins which are hormones that have mood boosting effects. Warm up before you start exercising and cool down after the activity is over. Hydrate yourself properly and have a healthy diet. Don't overdo any activity and use weights which are suitable for you.
