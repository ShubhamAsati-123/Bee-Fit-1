AEROBIC EXERCISE

There are many different types of aerobic exercises like walking,running,swimming,biking,dancing,rowing,cross-country skiing etc.Some of the benefits of aerobic exercises are like immproving cardiovascular health, reducing risk of chronic diseases,helping you lose weight or maintain a healthy weight,improving mood and mental health,strengthening bones and muscles,improving balance and coordination,increasing energy levels,improving sleep quality and reducing stress levels.
